# Alien Invaders MVP

## Goal
Deliver a playable 1980s-inspired Space Invaders tribute with a complete game loop and signature neon presentation.

## Must Have
- Player ship movement, shooting, and lives
- Alien formation movement, shooting, and waves
- Shield blocks that degrade on impact
- UFO/mothership bonus target
- Score, wave, and lives HUD
- Keyboard controls (A/D, arrows, space, shift, P, R)
- Responsive layout for desktop and mobile

## Nice to Have
- Audio synth effects
- Particle explosions
- Overcharge shot meter
- CRT scanline visuals

## Out of Scope
- Multiplayer
- Online leaderboard
- Mobile touch controls
